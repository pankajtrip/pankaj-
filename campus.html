Branches Offered at Government Polytechnic Nainital

Government Polytechnic Nainital offers various branches under the Diploma in Engineering program. These branches help students specialize in different fields of engineering and technology.

Information Technology

Students learn about the latest technologies in information systems and networks.

Mechanical Engineering

Mechanical engineering involves the design, analysis, and manufacturing of mechanical systems.

Electrical Engineering

This branch covers the study of electrical systems, circuits, and power generation.

Civil Engineering

Civil engineering focuses on the design, construction, and maintenance of infrastructure projects.

Electronics and Communication Engineering

This branch deals with the design and development of electronic systems and communication devices.
